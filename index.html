About Light Shield

Denver's premier chemical-free sanitization partner.

We deliver hospital-grade sterilization for residential, hospitality, and commercial environments. Our mobile experts deploy UV-C lighting arrays, ozone finishing, and advanced monitoring to disinfect spaces, neutralize odors, and eliminate pests in a single visit.

Founded in Denver, Light Shield is on a mission to revolutionize sanitization with technology that protects the people and properties that matter most—without exposing them to harsh chemicals or extended downtime.

Technology

UV-C light tuned for maximum pathogen kill-rate.

Light Shield’s proprietary systems emit UV-C light at the ideal 265 nm wavelength—proven to destroy pathogens without leaving residue. Combined with real-time dosimetry, we ensure every surface receives the correct exposure.

  • Chemical-free sterilization with zero residue or rinse requirements.
  • Rapid treatments completed in 1–3 hours with immediate re-occupancy.
  • Compatible with furniture, electronics, and sensitive materials.
  • Scalable for single rooms, entire properties, or recurring schedules.
